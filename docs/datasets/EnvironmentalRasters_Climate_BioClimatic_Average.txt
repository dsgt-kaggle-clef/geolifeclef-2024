The 19 CHELSA bioclimatic GeoTIFF rasters covering Europe. The resolution is 30 arc seconds, i.e. approximately 1km.
These 19 variables are standard in species distribution modeling and are derived from the monthly mean, max, mean temperature, and mean precipitation values and averaged over the period 1981-2010.
The description and transformation of the original variables into the raster values are described on the CHELSA webpage (https://chelsa-climate.org/bioclim/).

The global rasters were downloaded from Chelsa on november 2022 and cropped to the extent of GLC2023 study area.
Coordinate system is WGS84.
